Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days within the continental US. Express shipping (2-3 business days) and Next Day Delivery options are also available at checkout. International shipping times vary by location but generally take 7-14 business days.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer. Once we receive your return, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

How do I determine the right size?

We provide detailed size charts on each product page. For human clothing, measurements include chest, waist, and length. For dog clothing, we provide measurements for length, chest girth, and weight recommendations. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up.

Are your products true to size?

Yes, our products are designed to be true to standard US sizing. However, some styles (like oversized shirts) are intentionally cut larger. Always check the product description and size chart for specific fit information. If you're unsure, feel free to contact our customer service team for guidance.

How do I care for my GoteZ clothing?

For best results, machine wash cold with like colors, inside out. Tumble dry on low heat or hang to dry. Do not iron directly on printed designs. For dog clothing, hand washing is recommended to maintain shape and extend the life of the garment.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for qualified retailers and volume discounts for bulk orders. Please contact our sales team at wholesale@gotez.com with your business information and order requirements for more information.
