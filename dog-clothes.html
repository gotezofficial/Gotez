Dog Size Guide

Finding the right size for your furry friend is essential for comfort and style. Use our size guide to ensure a perfect fit for your dog.

To measure your dog, use a soft measuring tape and measure around the widest part of their chest (just behind the front legs). Then measure from the base of their neck to the base of their tail for length.

Size Chest (inches) Length (inches) Weight (lbs)
XS 10-12 8-10 3-6
S 13-16 11-13 7-12
M 17-20 14-16 13-22
L 21-25 17-20 23-45
XL 26-30 21-24 46-60
