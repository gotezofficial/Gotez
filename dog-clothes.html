Dog Size Guide

Not sure which size to choose? Use our handy size guide to find the perfect fit for your furry friend.

Size Weight Length (Back) Chest Girth Example Breeds
XS 2-4 lbs 6-8 inches 10-12 inches Teacup Chihuahua, Toy Poodle (puppy)
S 5-10 lbs 8-12 inches 12-16 inches Chihuahua, Pomeranian, Yorkshire Terrier
M 11-25 lbs 12-16 inches 16-20 inches Jack Russell, Beagle, Pug
L 26-40 lbs 16-20 inches 20-24 inches Border Collie, Bulldog, Cocker Spaniel
XL 41-65 lbs 20-24 inches 24-30 inches Labrador, Golden Retriever, German Shepherd

Measuring Tips:

  • Length: Measure from the base of the neck to the base of the tail along the back.
  • Chest: Measure the widest part of the chest, just behind the front legs.
  • When in doubt: If your dog is between sizes, we recommend sizing up.
