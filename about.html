Our Story

GoteZ was born from a simple idea: clothing should be as bold and unapologetic as the people who wear it. Founded in 2022 by a group of friends who were tired of bland, cookie-cutter fashion, we set out to create a brand that celebrates individuality, humor, and self-expression.

What started as a small operation printing edgy t-shirts in a garage has quickly grown into a thriving online business with customers worldwide. Our designs range from darkly humorous to provocatively funny, always with the goal of making a statement and starting conversations.

We believe that fashion should be fun, and that sometimes the best way to deal with life's challenges is to laugh at them. That's why our clothing features designs that push boundaries and aren't afraid to be a little controversial.

Our Process

Every GoteZ product starts with an idea â€“ usually something that makes us laugh or raises an eyebrow. Our in-house design team then transforms these concepts into bold, eye-catching graphics that look great on our premium quality apparel.

We source our materials from ethical suppliers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Each item is screen printed by hand in our workshop, ensuring attention to detail and consistent quality across all our products.

Before any design goes into production, we test it extensively to ensure the print won't fade or crack after washing. We're committed to creating products that not only look great when you first get them but continue to do so after many wears and washes.

Our Values

Humor First

We believe in the power of humor to connect people and make life more enjoyable. Our designs aim to make you and others laugh, even if it's sometimes at the expense of social norms.

Quality Always

We never compromise on the quality of our products. From the fabric we use to the printing techniques we employ, everything is chosen to ensure our clothing lasts and remains comfortable.

Community Driven

Our customers are part of a community that appreciates edgy humor and isn't afraid to express themselves. We value this community and regularly incorporate customer feedback and ideas.
